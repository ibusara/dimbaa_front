Kiwango cha Tathimini ya Ugumu wa Mchezo

Kiwango cha ugumu wa Mchezo kuwiana na alama anazoshinda mwamuzi. Tathmini ifanywe kwa kila mwamuzi binafsi katika mchezo na si kwa umoja wao. (Level of difficulty must be intergrated into the mark and should be assessed for each Match official Separately

1 Mchezo mgumu sana wenye matukio mengi magumu kwa Mwamuzi (Very difficult Match with many difficulty situationsfor the official)
2 Mchezo mgumu ukiwa na sehemu ya matukio magumu kufanya maamuzi (Difficult Match with some difficult decisions for the official)
3 Mchezo wa Kawaida kwa Mwamuzi; changamoto chache za kimchezo (Normal match for the official; few challenging situations)
